Why choose EcoCourierKo?

We connect eco-conscious couriers with communities in Davao to collect and recycle electronic waste responsibly. Together, we reduce pollution and promote a sustainable city.

How It Works

  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Schedule a convenient e-waste pickup.
  3. Our volunteer courier collects your e-waste.
  4. E-waste is delivered to certified recycling partners.

Our Impact

  • 120+ pickups completed
  • 500kg e-waste recycled
  • 30+ active volunteers

About EcoCourierKo

EcoCourierKo is a volunteer-driven platform that connects communities in Davao with couriers who collect and deliver e-waste responsibly for recycling and proper disposal. Together, we make Davao cleaner and greener.
