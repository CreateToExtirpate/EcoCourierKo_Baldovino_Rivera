Why choose EcoCourierKo?

We want to promote responsible e-waste disposal in Davao by providing a convenient and eco-friendly solution. Our platform aims to harbor a community within Davao that values sustainability and environmental responsibility through the proper collection and disposal of electronic wastes. Together, we can create a cleaner and greener Davao!

How It Works

  1. Sign-up or log into your account.
  2. Create a pickup order for your 5kg+ e-waste.
  3. An EcoCourier will accept the order and schedule a date to pick it up.
  4. Give your e-waste to our EcoCourier, and he/she will deliver it to the city's sanitary landfill.

About EcoCourierKo

EcoCourierKo is a volunteer-driven platform that connects communities in Davao with couriers who collect and deliver e-waste responsibly for recycling and proper disposal. Together, we make Davao cleaner and greener.
